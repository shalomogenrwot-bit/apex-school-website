Our History

Apex Nursery and Primary School was founded in 2015 by Mr. Oburu, a visionary educator who believed in the power of holistic education. Starting with just 50 students and 5 teachers in a small building, we have grown into one of the most respected educational institutions in the region.

Over the past 15 years, we have expanded our campus to include state-of-the-art facilities, developed comprehensive academic programs, and built a community of over 1,200 students and 120 dedicated faculty members.
